PHANTOM

THE PHANTOM OF SPARTE

Strength, courage, and masculinity are all embodied in a magnificent spartan musk scent.

A smell that perfectly embodies the spirit and soul of the legendary warriors.


masculinity O Strength O courage


description


The perfume begins with a burst of lemon and bergamot that is reminiscent of the crisp mountain air.

A strong and robust musk note that evokes the fortitude and tenacity of the Spartan warriors dominates the fragrance's core.

Warm spices like cinnamon and nutmeg are used to accentuate the musk note, giving the fragrance a spark of ardor.

The earthy vetiver, woodsy cedar, and smoky incense in the base notes evoke memories of the old Spartan forests and rites.
